Coffee Futures Market Weekly Update

August 30, 2025

Market Overview

August coffee market surged to 386.10 cents driven by tariff shock and supply shortage concerns with massive speculative inflows (CFTC Managed Money +4,000 contracts), but entered short-term correction phase due to Brazil harvest completion (Arabica 98%, Robusta 100%) and technical overbought signals.

Key Highlights

  • CFTC Data: Managed Money net longs surged +4,000 contracts
  • Brazil Harvest: Arabica 98% complete, Robusta 100%
  • ICE Stock: Arabica 717,113 bags, Robusta 6,614 lots - both historically low
  • Tariff Impact: US 50% tariff remains structural bullish factor
  • Technical: Overbought signals suggest short-term correction

Outlook

Short-term correction expected near 350 cent support. Structural bullish factors (ICE stock shortage, US tariff impact, Volcafe deficit forecast) support medium-term upside. 2026 flowering period will be key variable.
