US Brazil Tariff Shock: Market Crossroads

In-depth Analysis

August 11, 2025

Executive Summary

With the US imposing an unprecedented 50% tariff on all Brazilian imports, the global coffee market stands at a crossroads where two powerful opposing forces collide: Brazil supply seeking alternative markets vs ICE futures CTD (Cheapest-to-Deliver) mechanism recalibration.

Key Market Forces

  • Downward Pressure: Brazil supply surplus seeking new markets (China, EU)
  • Upward Pressure: ICE 'C' futures CTD recalibration (Peru, Honduras becoming CTD)
  • Supply Chain Shift: US roasters diversifying to non-Brazil origins
  • Price Divergence: Spot vs futures gap potential

Strategic Implications

Watch for potential spot-futures price divergence due to declining futures trading appeal for Brazil coffee. Alternative origins (Peru, Honduras, Colombia) will benefit from US demand shift.
