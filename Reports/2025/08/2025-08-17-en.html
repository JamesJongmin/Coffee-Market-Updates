Perfect Storm: Coffee Price Surge Deep Dive

Analysis of H2 2025 Market Dynamics

August 17, 2025

The Perfect Storm

The coffee market is experiencing a "perfect storm" as multiple bullish factors converge simultaneously: climate change impacts on Brazil, US tariff shock, and structural supply deficit.

Converging Factors

  • Climate Change: Brazil drought worst in decades, Vietnam weather volatility
  • US Tariff: 50% tariff effective Aug 6 disrupting traditional supply flows
  • Supply Deficit: 5th consecutive year of Arabica shortage projected
  • ICE Stocks: Multi-year lows creating delivery squeeze potential
  • Demand Growth: China and emerging markets driving consumption growth

Implications

This perfect storm suggests structurally higher coffee prices for the foreseeable future. Market participants should prepare for elevated volatility and price levels.
