Coffee Futures Market Weekly Update

August 16, 2025

Market Overview

September Arabica coffee (KCU25) closed at 341.65 cents, recording 6.33% monthly gain. US 50% tariff on Brazil coffee (effective Aug 1) and Cerrado Mineiro frost combined to maximize supply concerns.

Key Highlights

  • Price: KCU25 at 341.65 cents (+6.33% monthly, +33.61% YoY)
  • US Tariff: 50% tariff effective Aug 1 - major supply shock
  • Brazil Frost: Cerrado Mineiro frost adding to supply concerns
  • ICE Stock: Near multi-year lows
  • CFTC: Net longs increased to 22,466 contracts

Outlook

Bullish momentum supported by tariff impact and weather concerns. Watch for Brazil flowering season developments.
