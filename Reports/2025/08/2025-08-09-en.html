Coffee Futures Market Weekly Update

August 9, 2025

Market Overview

Coffee futures surged to 308.95 cents on Brazil 50% tariff implementation (Aug 6) and Minas Gerais weather concerns. Closing at +3.74% daily on Aug 8, weekly gain of 8.7%.

Key Highlights

  • Price: 308.95 cents (+8.7% weekly)
  • Tariff: Brazil 50% tariff effective Aug 6
  • China: Approved 183 new Brazil coffee companies - trade flow shift
  • Weather: Minas Gerais concerns adding to upside
  • Impact: US coffee prices expected to rise 15-20%

Outlook

Global supply chain diversification accelerating. Bullish momentum likely to continue as tariff impact materializes.
