Standing Against the Giant Wave

Global Coffee Market Structural Changes

July 25, 2025

Executive Summary

The global coffee market faces unprecedented price rises and volatility due to structural changes. With US-Brazil 50% tariff just 1 week away, both Arabica and Robusta markets are experiencing fundamental shifts.

Structural Drivers

  • US-Brazil Tariff: 50% tariff effective Aug 1
  • Arabica All-Time High: Historical price levels sustained
  • Robusta Renaissance: Vietnam and Indonesia supply dynamics
  • Brazil Climate: Drought and potential frost risks
  • Vietnam Durian: Farmers shifting from coffee to durian

Strategic Implications

Prepare for new market paradigm with structurally higher prices and increased volatility. Supply chain diversification essential.
