Coffee Crisis: 50% Tariff Shock Analysis

Chain Effects on US Market and Consumers

July 16, 2025

Executive Summary

Trump administration's 50% tariff on Brazilian coffee (effective Aug 1, 2025) could spike US coffee futures (KC) prices to 355.5-385.0 cents short-term through 'Cheapest-to-Deliver (CTD)' reshuffling mechanism.

Price Impact Analysis

  • Short-term Target: 355.5-385.0 cents
  • CTD Shift: Peru, Honduras become cheapest delivery options
  • Supply Chain: Major disruption to US coffee supply
  • Consumer Impact: US retail prices expected to surge

Strategic Response

Roasters and traders should prepare for supply chain restructuring. Diversification to non-Brazil origins essential.
