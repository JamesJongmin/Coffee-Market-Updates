Coffee Futures Price Prediction Model

Statistical Analysis

July 29, 2025

Model Overview

Multiple linear regression analysis confirms USD/BRL exchange rate has dominant influence, explaining 84.3% of total price variation. Model achieves 79.5% prediction accuracy.

Key Variables

  • USD/BRL Rate: 84.3% explanatory power - dominant factor
  • CFTC Positions: Speculative positioning secondary factor
  • NDVI Index: Crop health indicator
  • Dollar Index: Additional explanatory variable

Implications

Brazil's 40% global coffee production share manifests in exchange rate-centric pricing mechanism. Watch BRL movements for price direction signals.
