Coffee Market Surge: The Compressed Spring Explodes

Perfect Storm of Supply Shortage and Geopolitical Tensions

September 16, 2025

52-Week High Achieved

ICE Arabica futures surged to 417.65 cents per pound on September 15, setting a new 52-week high. This represents the culmination of multiple bullish forces converging to create a "compressed spring" scenario in the market.

Bullish Catalysts

  • Brazil Weather: Severe drought during flowering period threatens 2025/26 crop
  • ICE Stock Depletion: Certified stocks at 16-month low
  • US Tariff Impact: 50% tariff on Brazil disrupting traditional supply flows
  • Structural Deficit: 5th consecutive year of Arabica supply shortage projected
  • Speculative Momentum: Managed money net longs at elevated levels

Price Targets

Short-term: 440 cents (February high retest)

Extreme Scenario: 520 cents if supply shocks intensify

Support: 380 cents (structural floor based on deficit fundamentals)
