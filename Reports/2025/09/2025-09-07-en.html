Coffee Futures: Volatility Seeking Equilibrium

Speculative Overheating vs Fundamental Tightening

September 7, 2025

Market Assessment

Coffee market shows 2-week consolidation in 370-375 cent range after August's 31% surge. The market is balancing strong bullish factors against bearish pressures.

Bullish Factors

  • Brazil Arabica production -11.2% (Conab revision)
  • US 50% tariff disrupting supply chain
  • ICE stocks at multi-year lows
  • 5th consecutive year of Arabica deficit

Bearish Factors

  • Record Robusta harvest in Vietnam
  • Speculative net longs at 35,708 contracts (elevated)
  • Brazil harvest completion reducing near-term concerns

Scenario Outlook

Most Likely (60%): Volatile range trading at 350-410 cents

Bullish (25%): Break above 410 toward 450 cents

Bearish (15%): Correction to 320-340 cents
