Coffee Futures Market Weekly Update

September 13, 2025

Market Overview

December Arabica coffee (KCZ25) rose 2.78% to 396.85 cents as the US 50% tariff impact materializes. Brazil exports have declined for 9 consecutive months, confirming supply disruption.

Key Highlights

  • Price: KCZ25 closed at 396.85 cents (+2.78%)
  • Brazil Exports: August exports down 17.5% YoY to 3.14M bags
  • US Exports Plunge: Brazil-to-US exports down 46%
  • ICE Trading Costs: Margin increases reflecting volatility
  • BRL Strength: Strong Real supporting price floor

Outlook

Tariff-driven supply disruption creating structural tightness. Short-term target: 400-420 cents.
