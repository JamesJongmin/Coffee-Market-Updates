Supply-Demand Analysis

The coffee futures market continues to be driven by the intersection of tight supply fundamentals and tariff-related uncertainties. ICE certified Arabica stocks have declined sharply since the August 6 implementation of 50% US tariffs on Brazilian coffee, as US importers cancel contracts and Brazilian volumes are diverted to alternative markets including China and the EU.

Volcafe's projection of an 8.5 million bag Arabica deficit for 2025/26 (5th consecutive year) remains the structural support for prices. Meanwhile, Brazil's ongoing drought during the critical flowering period raises concerns about 2026/27 crop prospects.
